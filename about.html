About Us

New England Hindu Association (NEHA) Inc. is a non-profit religious organization dedicated to humanitarian and charitable purposes.

Mission:

Our mission is to preserve and promote the rich cultural, religious, literary, and charitable traditions of Bengal, while fostering a sense of community and cultural awareness among our members and the wider community.

Purpose:

The primary purposes of the New England Hindu Association (NEHA) Inc. include, but are not limited to:

  • Fostering fellowship and community through social gatherings.
  • Encouraging social interaction among members through festivals, dramas, dances, musicals, and other forms of cultural entertainment.
  • Engaging in activities that promote the social, cultural, and psychological well-being of our members.

Activities:

The New England Hindu Association (NEHA) Inc. organizes a variety of social and cultural events, including but not limited to:

  • Parades/food festivals
  • Bangla New Year celebrations
  • Stage dramas
  • Cultural programs
  • Hindu religious festivals

Local community members, particularly those of Bangladeshi and Asian immigrant backgrounds, take part in our events, with opportunities for children to showcase their native culture and language through poetry, folk music, and classical dance. Events are typically held in city-permitted public schools, parks, libraries, and public/private rental halls in Connecticut. Activities are organized around significant occasions, including Hindu religious festivals, Bangla New Year, and national days, with approximately 6 to 10 events held annually. Local community businesses and participant donations help fund our events. Our members volunteer their time and efforts, often outside of their regular jobs, to serve the organization without any monetary compensation.
